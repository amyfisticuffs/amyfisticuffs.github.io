Experience

    In 2012, I launched my own company and contracted with STR where I implemented user interfaces to facilitate the visualization and understanding of dynamic, complex datasets.

  2.

    1999 - 2011

    Senior Software Engineer
    BBN Technologies, Cambridge, MA

    During my years at BBN I designed and implemented a Java web-services-based distributed framework for real-time information assurance assessment during transactions, enhancing the security posture of information systems. I also worked on a healthcare initiative where I developed a UI to support the efficient exploration and organization of medical record data from disparate heterogenous sources for outcome-based analytics. STARS is now being used in many TRACONs around the United States. At the beginning of my Raytheon career I teamed with Systems Engineers to design an interface to collect and display information from a time-critical distributed battle simulation test-bed.
